About

Parkinghood is a marketplace based on the collaborative consumption that allows you to rent and share private parking spaces on a short-term basis. At Parkinghood we have a double mission:

1. Parking doesn't have to be neither stressful nor expensive current prices are not justified since the problem is not the lack of spaces, but their underuse. For this reason, we have created a low-cost alternative based on the collaborative parking.

2. Redistribution among citizens urban parking generates profits which are only distributed among a few. To change that, we are already increasing the offer of available parking spaces in the cities and generating additional income for our hosts.

Benefits


  • Save time and money

    Mr. Driver, stop going in circles because here you will finally find your place. Through Parkinghood you can book a parking space close to your destination at a reasonable price. Join us and start saving!
